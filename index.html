Spot.Me: Smarter Lifting

Here's why Spot.Me is for you:

Create Programs

Whether you're working out once per week or you're doing two-a-days, Spot.Me has you covered. Our streamlined training program creation tool allows you to quickly make and customize nearly any program, or enjoy out-of-the-box support for popular strength and hypertrophy programs like Starting Strength, Stronglifts 5x5, 5/3/1 and many more.

Log Workouts

Spot.Me features a gorgeous, focused workout-in-progress screen that allows you to quickly log your sets, reps, or any other training metrics you track. Your time is precious, and with Spot.Me you'll be able to spend more time enjoying your training and less time fliddling with your phone or a clumsy notebook.

Visualize Progress

You work hard to achieve your goals, and Spot.Me keeps you on track by visualizing the fruits of your labour through beautiful graphs. Wondering how much your clean & jerk max varies from workout to workout, week to week, or month to month? Or whether your squats have improved relative to your bodyweight over the course of your previous training program? Spot.Me has you covered.
